CCGvu

CCGvu is a program for plotting and curve fitting of the NOAA/CMDL trace gas measurements. Measurement species supported are CO2, CH4, CO, H2, and the C13 and O18 isotope measurements of CO2. Curve fits are based on a combination of a fit to the data using a function with polynomial plus annual harmonic terms, and filtering of the residuals in the frequency domain.
